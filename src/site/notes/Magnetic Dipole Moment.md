The [[Magnetic Dipole\|magnetic dipole]] moment is a measure of the **strength** of the [[Magnetic Field\|magnetic field]].
$$
\vec{m}  =NIA\vec{a}_z
$$
number of loops $N$ times the current $I$ times the area the loop encloses $A$.
