The [[Torque\|torque]] for a [[Magnetic Dipole\|magnetic dipole]] is 
$$
\vec{T}=\vec{m}\times\vec{B}
$$
where $\vec{m}$ is the [[Magnetic Dipole Moment\|Magnetic Dipole Moment]] and $\vec{B}$ is the [[Magnetic Field\|Magnetic Field]]
