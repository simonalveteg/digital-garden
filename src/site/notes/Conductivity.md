Conductivity is defined as $$
\sigma=-\rho_e\mu_e
	$$ where $\mu_e$ is the [[Mobilitet\|mobility]] and $\rho_e$ is the electron [[Charge Density\|charge density]].

SI-enhet Siemens/meter. [S/m]
