$$\epsilon = \frac{nytta}{pris} = \frac{W_{netto}}{Q_{in}} = \frac{Q_{in}-|Q_{ut}|}{Q_{in}} = 1 -\frac{|Q_{ut}|}{Q_{in}} $$
