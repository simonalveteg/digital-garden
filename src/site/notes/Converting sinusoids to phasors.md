A [[Phasors\|phasor]] contains the amplitude and phase shift of the original signal. If we have a sinusoid
$$
7cos(2t+30\degree)
$$
the [[Phasors\|phasor]] representation would be
$$
7e^{ j30\degree }
$$
in the same way
$$
-4\sin(10t+10\degree)
$$
corresponds to 
$$
4e^{ j(10+90)\degree }
$$
